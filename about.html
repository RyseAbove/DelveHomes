The Founding

This is our CEO, Lorem Ipsum. He is currently 81 years old. He founded DelveHomes when he was only 27 years old in 1968. He has been running the company ever since.

His first prototype airship was nicknamed, “The Flying Thing.” He built it in 1965, when he was 24 years old. Recognized from locals to international levels. It has been stored in The Museum of Technical History since 1995.
